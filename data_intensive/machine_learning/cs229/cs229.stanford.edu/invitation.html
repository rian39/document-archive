Overview

CS229 is a graduate-level introduction to machine learning and pattern recognition. Students engage in a quarter-long project of their choosing. Projects range from developing novel machine learning algorithms to applying machine learning to current research and industry problems. The list of projects below shows the amazing range of problems for which machine learning can improve performance. We encourage you to come see the posters and discuss with students how machine learning can impact your areas of interest.
